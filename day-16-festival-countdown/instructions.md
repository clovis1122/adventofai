# Day 14: Social Media Blitz

## Instructions
Create a simple, beautiful countdown app that shows:

- Days, hours, minutes, seconds until next year's festival (December 1, 2026, 10:00 AM)
- A winter-themed design
- Fun facts about the festival that rotate
- A "notify me" email signup (just the form and UI)

Fun Facts You Can Use:
- "Last year's festival had 5,000 visitors!"
- "We served 2,000 cups of hot cocoa!"
- "The ice sculpture took 12 hours to carve!"
- "50 volunteers made this festival possible!"
- "The yeti mascot won by popular vote!"
